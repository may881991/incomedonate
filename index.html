THE LONGEST BURSARY

A project by OrangeAid

When it comes to providing an education for the next generation, every dollar counts. And every donor who contribute to the cause does too. This is why Income OrangeAid introduces the “Longest Bursary” initiative.

The Longest Bursary initiative, being part of Income’s OrangeAid Future Development Programme (FDP), will support tertiary students from the lowest income households through partnerships with all polytechnics and the Institute of Technical Education (ITE) in Singapore. The FDP provides financial assistance to youth-in-need through bursaries to help them stay in school and level their playing field with financial literacy training, as well as, personal and career guidance. In doing so, we hope these youths gain the confidence, belief and skills to thrive and secure a better future for themselves and their families.
